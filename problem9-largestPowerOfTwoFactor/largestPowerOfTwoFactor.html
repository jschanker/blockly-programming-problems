Largest Power of Two Factor

Largest Power of Two Factor: Did you conquer the lgCeil problem yet? Relishing another challenge? Sure you are. OK, we're giving you the same setup, but this time you'll be given nonzero integers and you want to return the largest power of two that's a factor of the number. For example, largestPowerOfTwoFactorThatIsAFactorOf(120) should return 8 because 8 is the largest power of two that's a factor of 120 while largestPowerOfTwoFactorThatIsAFactorOf(75) should return 1. Remember the powers of two (with nonnegative integer exponents) are 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64, 128, 256, 512, 1024, etc. Of those in the list, 120 is only divisible by 1, 2, 4, and 8 while the only number in this list that divides any odd number such as 75 is 1.
